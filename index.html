About us

Merkato Ethiopian Food Store has been owned and operated by Yadi Benti since 2008. It it not only a place to find authentic Ethiopian dishes and ingredients, but also a place for community to gather. You will find all you need at Merkato and Yadi will always be there to greet you with a friendly smile.
